Pricing

Pact charges a small platform fee on each completed transaction. There are no listing fees, no subscription costs, and a full refund if a pact is cancelled before work begins.

Platform Fee
5% + $0.25

A platform fee of 5% + $0.25 applies to both sides of every completed transaction — charged to the client on top of the agreed price as a platform fee, and deducted from the specialist's payout. Card processing is grossed up so the platform absorbs no processing costs. Tax is added where applicable and varies by location.

Example — $100 agreed price (no tax):

Total charged$108.70 + tax
Specialist receives

Card processing fees are estimates based on standard Braintree rates and vary by card type. Tax is calculated at checkout based on the applicable rate for your location. Specialists must connect a payout account via Braintree to receive funds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pact available on iOS and Android?

Yes. Pact is available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

What happens if I'm not happy with the work?

Don't release the funds. Instead, raise a dispute through the app and submit your evidence. Pact will review both sides and issue a resolution — either releasing payment to the specialist or refunding the full amount to you.

When does the specialist get paid?

Once the client releases the funds or the 48-hour auto-release window passes after proof submission. Funds are released from escrow and transferred to the specialist's connected payout account via Braintree.

What if a pact is cancelled?

Either party can cancel before proof is submitted. The escrowed payment is refunded in full to the client's original payment method — typically within 5–10 business days. Once proof has been submitted, cancellation is no longer available — only release or dispute.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All payments are processed by Braintree — a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor (a PayPal service). Pact never stores card numbers. Payment is held in escrow at seal time and released to the specialist only when the work is confirmed complete.

What can be listed on Pact?

Anything legal — services like home repairs, cleaning, tutoring, creative work, and tech help, as well as physical goods and locally sourced items.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@sunexplode.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

Policies

Cancellation Policy

Either party may cancel a Pact at any time before proof of completion is submitted. The escrowed payment is refunded in full to the client's original payment method. Once a specialist has submitted proof of completion, cancellation is no longer available — the client must either release the funds or raise a dispute.

Refund Policy

Pact is a marketplace platform and does not itself sell goods or services — all transactions are arranged directly between users. Refunds are issued in the following circumstances:

  • A Pact is cancelled before proof submission — the full escrowed amount is refunded to the client's original payment method.
  • A dispute is raised and resolved in the client's favour — the full pact amount is refunded to the original payment method.
  • A pact is voided due to a platform enforcement action — any escrowed funds are refunded in full.

Refunds are processed through Braintree and typically appear within 5–10 business days depending on the card issuer. To request a refund outside of the above scenarios, contact support@sunexplode.com.

Dispute Resolution Policy

If a client is unsatisfied with the completed work, either party may raise a dispute through the app before releasing payment. Both parties are required to submit evidence. Pact reviews the evidence and issues one of the following resolutions:

  • In favour of the specialist — payment is released from escrow and transferred to the specialist.
  • In favour of the client — the full pact amount is refunded to the client.

Dispute decisions are final. For escalations, contact support@sunexplode.com. Users who repeatedly raise frivolous disputes may have their accounts suspended.

Return Policy

Pact does not directly sell goods or services and has no return policy of its own. Any return or exchange arrangement for physical goods transacted between users is solely between those users. Pact's dispute resolution process covers payment disputes — not physical returns or exchanges.

Off-Platform Payments

Arranging payment outside of the Pact platform is strictly prohibited and may result in a permanent ban. Pact's escrow-based payment system exists specifically to protect both parties — bypassing it removes the dispute resolution, refund guarantees, and payment security that the platform is designed to provide. All transactions arranged through Pact must be completed through the app.

Legal & Export Restrictions

The platform may not be used to arrange transactions for goods or services that are illegal under applicable law in the user's jurisdiction. Users are solely responsible for ensuring their listings and activities comply with all applicable laws, including any licensing, permit, or insurance requirements relevant to what they offer. Pact does not facilitate the sale or export of controlled goods, weapons, hazardous materials, or any item subject to US export control regulations (EAR/ITAR).
