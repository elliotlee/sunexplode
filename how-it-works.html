HOW IT WORKS

Pact is a local marketplace where people hire and get hired — with payment held securely until the job is done.

Two Ways to Use Pact

POST A WANTED AD
You need something done. Write what you need, set your budget, and specialists apply. Review them, pick one, and seal the deal.
POST A CLASSIFIED
You offer a service. Post what you do and your rate. Clients browse and send you a request directly.

How a Pact Works

1
SEAL IT
Both parties agree on the job and price. Payment locks into the Vault — a secure hold that guarantees the specialist gets paid and the client gets the work done first.
2
DO THE WORK
The specialist completes the job and submits proof — a photo or written description showing it's done.
3
HONOR IT
The client confirms the work and releases payment. If they don't respond within 48 hours of proof being submitted, payment releases automatically.
4
RATE EACH OTHER
Both parties leave a rating. Ratings are submitted privately and revealed at the same time.

The Vault

When a Pact is sealed, the agreed amount is held securely — not paid yet, not refundable yet. It releases to the specialist only when the work is confirmed complete. This protects both sides.

If Something Goes Wrong

Either party can raise a dispute while the Pact is active. Pact reviews the case and makes a final call — funds are released, refunded, or split. The decision is final within the app.
